With the latest technologies, we've been able to create a way for retailers to more easily track,
and control their stock, and to be able to notify when their product(s) come back in stock.

By combining NFC, GPS, and barcodes together, information about products can be stored quicker and more securely.
With this information, this means that you can use many filters that are suited to your needs.

Need to save money? Find some specific brand of food? Or something as ridiculous as finding a sofa?
Anything, we'll help get you covered, and if you don't see what you want, put it in our Notify list. You'll see it before you know it!
